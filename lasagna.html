Lasagna Recipe

Description

This delicious lasagna is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and add tomato sauce.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish: noodles, meat sauce, cheese mixture, and repeat.
  5. Bake in the oven until bubbly and golden brown.
